Elon Musk

Born: June 28, 1971

Nationality: South African, Canadian, American

Occupation: Entrepreneur, Investor, Engineer

Early Life

Elon Reeve Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. He developed an interest in computing and technology at an early age.

Career

Musk moved to the U.S. to attend the University of Pennsylvania. He co-founded Zip2, which was sold to Compaq. He then founded X.com, which became PayPal after a merger and was sold to eBay.

Companies

Musk is known for founding SpaceX, Tesla Inc., The Boring Company, Neuralink, and co-founding OpenAI. His ventures span electric vehicles, space exploration, neurotechnology, and infrastructure.

Personal Life

Elon Musk has been married multiple times and has several children. He is known for his ambitious vision of the future, including colonizing Mars.
